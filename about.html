About Us

Winnipeg’s
Trusted Experts

Detail, Precision, and Passion Combined

At Nawab Tint, we’re more than just a tint shop—we’re passionate car care professionals dedicated to enhancing the look, comfort, and performance of your vehicle. Proudly serving Winnipeg and surrounding areas, our team specializes in premium window tinting, automotive detailing, headlight restoration, and light tinting services. With years of hands-on experience, we focus on delivering exceptional quality, unmatched attention to detail, and results that exceed expectations.
We use high-grade materials including ceramic and carbon films, and advanced tools to ensure every vehicle leaves our shop looking its absolute best. Whether you’re protecting your car from harmful UV rays, restoring cloudy headlights, or giving your ride a full interior and exterior refresh, Nawab Tint is committed to giving you top-tier service at competitive prices.

Meet The Founder

Dilbag Aulakh

Owner

Dilbag Aulakh is the visionary behind Nawab Tint, bringing years of industry expertise and a relentless drive for quality to the world of window tinting. His journey began with a passion for automotive detailing and customization, which naturally evolved into a focused craft in high-performance tinting. Through hard work, dedication, and a commitment to excellence, Dilbagh has built Nawab Tint into a trusted name for both vehicle and residential tint services.

What sets Dilbag apart is his hands-on approach. He doesn’t just run the business—he's involved in every detail, from film selection and installation to quality control and customer service. His goal has always been simple: deliver clean, long-lasting results while providing each client with a smooth, professional experience. Over the years, his consistent work ethic and friendly attitude have earned him a loyal customer base and a reputation for reliability.
