Headlight & Taillight Tinting in Winnipeg

At Nawab Tint, we specialize in headlight and taillight tinting to give your car a sleek, customized look while adding a layer of protection. Whether you want a subtle smoke or a bold blackout finish, we offer professional-grade tint films and expert installation in Winnipeg.

Enhance your vehicle’s style and protection with precision tinting for your headlights and taillights.

What Is Light Tinting?

Light tinting involves applying a thin, transparent film over your headlights or taillights to change their appearance, protect the lenses, and reduce glare. We use heat-applied, stretchable films that mold perfectly to the shape of your lights, giving a factory-finish look every time.

Benefits

  • Instantly gives your car a sportier, cleaner, and more modern look.
  • Prevents scratches, oxidation, and UV fading over time.
  • Adds a tough protective layer against minor impacts and road debris.
  • Choose your tint shade to match your personality or car color.

Is headlight and taillight tinting legal in Winnipeg?

Yes, as long as the tint allows sufficient light to pass through for safe driving. We follow Manitoba’s guidelines to ensure your vehicle stays road-legal unless otherwise requested for show or off-road use.

What tint shades are available for headlights and taillights?

Light Smoke – Offers a clean, refined look with minimal darkening.
Medium Smoke – A perfect balance between style and visibility.
Dark Smoke / Blackout – Provides an aggressive, bold appearance, ideal for show cars or off-road use.

How long does it take to tint headlights or taillights?

The process typically takes 30 to 60 minutes per pair, depending on the vehicle type and tint choice. We ensure a precise, bubble-free finish every time.
