Interior & Exterior Detailing in Winnipeg

At Nawab Tint, we provide top-quality interior and exterior detailing to keep your vehicle looking its best. Whether it’s a quick refresh or a deep clean, our professional team uses high-end products and techniques to restore your car to its original condition. Serving Winnipeg with precision, we deliver excellent results for cars, trucks, and SUVs.

Bring your car back to life with our expert interior and exterior detailing services.

Interior Detailing Services

Our interior detailing service is designed to clean every nook and cranny of your vehicle’s cabin. We use specialized tools to remove dust, dirt, and debris from hard-to-reach areas, leaving your car’s interior spotless and refreshed. We also focus on sanitizing high-touch areas for your health and comfort.

What’s Included:

  • Vacuuming seats, carpets, and floor mats
  • Wiping down dashboard, door panels, and consoles
  • Deep cleaning of upholstery (fabric or leather)
  • Steam cleaning and stain removal
  • Window cleaning inside and out
  • Sanitization of high-touch surfaces
  • Air freshener to leave a clean scent

Exterior Detailing Services

Exterior detailing restores the shine and protection of your vehicle's body. We use top-of-the-line products to ensure your car is clean, shiny, and well-protected against environmental factors. From washing to waxing, our service adds protection against road salt, dirt, and UV rays.

What’s Included::

  • Hand wash and dry with microfiber towels
  • Paint correction (if needed) to remove scratches and swirl marks
  • Clay bar treatment to remove contaminants
  • Waxing or sealing for added protection
  • Tire and rim cleaning
  • Window and windshield cleaning

How long does it take to detail my vehicle?

The duration depends on the level of service requested. A basic interior or exterior detail typically takes 1-2 hours, while a complete detailing service can take 3-4 hours or more, depending on vehicle size and condition.

Do I need to book an appointment in advance for detailing?

Yes, we recommend booking in advance, especially for weekend or peak-time appointments. You can easily book by calling, texting, or by filling appointment form.

Can detailing remove scratches or dents from my car?

Detailing can help reduce the appearance of minor scratches and swirl marks through paint correction. However, deeper scratches or dents will require separate repair services.

How often should I get my car detailed?

For optimal vehicle maintenance, we recommend detailing your car at least every 3-6 months, or more often if exposed to harsh conditions, like frequent road salt or heavy traffic.
